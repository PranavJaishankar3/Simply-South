Some Words About Us

Welcome to Simply South, where we focus on the vegetarian cuisine of India. Our restaurant is a homage to the numerous spices, herbs, and vegetables that are cultivated in India. With our very carefully created dishes, we will show you what India looks like in just one bite. We invite you to join us on the journey through the world of vegetarian Indian cuisine.

What sets us apart is our commitment to showcasing all the flavorful dishes of India, without the use of meat. At Simply South, we use only the freshest of ingredients and top notch spices to create dishes that will explode in your mouth with their diverse flavors. Our chefs are masters in not only making the food delectable, but also nutritious.
